Instructions

You will see a scene with a partial description of it. It is your task to guess which of the children the description is referring to.


To make a guess, click on one of the children. Whenever you make a guess by clicking, more words of the description will appear.

Note: sometimes it will be impossible to determine which child the description is referring to until the second or third try and you will have to make a random guess. Good luck!
