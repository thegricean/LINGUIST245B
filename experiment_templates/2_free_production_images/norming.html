It was 9 years ago that you took your first step into a spaceship. Now your greatest dream actually came true: exploring the universe and its creatures. On your way through the unknown, you stop at every planet you see. There! That’s a new one - this greenish shimmering globe. Shortly after landing, you meet the first inhabitant of this planet: Daxy.

He is a kind and sweet creature from the species "Daxon". To welcome you on his planet, Daxy decides to cook a welcoming meal for the both of you.
Can you help Daxy choose the right ingredients?


Daxy is very grateful for your help.



Your job is to tell Daxy which of the food items are needed for the dish. One of the displayed items will have a green border around it; this is the one you need. Daxy understands English really well - he knows the names for all the fruits, vegetables, and colors, so you can use those. But his alien race doesn't know about locations like 'first', 'last', left', 'middle', or 'right', so avoid using those.

Tell Daxy which food item you need next.
